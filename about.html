Who We Are

Empowering Creativity & Preserving Memories

StudioMitra was born from a simple observation: finding a reliable, high-quality photography studio was often a complex and time-consuming process. We set out to change that.

Today, we are India's premier platform connecting clients with exceptional photographers. Our smart, AI-driven tools not only help clients discover top talent but also empower studio owners to streamline their workflows, from booking management to delivering stunning virtual photobooks.

Our Mission

To make professional photography services accessible, transparent, and seamless for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the global standard platform for the creative photography ecosystem.
